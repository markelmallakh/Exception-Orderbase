Frequently Asked Questions

Browse the shop, add your favourite cakes and sweets to the cart, then choose your delivery area and preferred time slot at checkout. You can pay online or select cash on delivery, and you will receive an SMS confirmation once your order is accepted.
We deliver across Greater Cairo, Giza, Alexandria and most major cities. Delivery fees start from EGP 35 and are calculated automatically based on your selected address at checkout. Orders above EGP 750 qualify for free delivery within Cairo and Giza.
Yes. For celebration and custom cakes we recommend ordering at least 48 hours in advance. At checkout you can pick a delivery date and a two-hour time window, and we will do our best to arrive right on schedule for your occasion.
Many of our oriental sweets contain nuts, and all products are prepared in kitchens that also handle nuts, dairy, gluten and sesame. If you have a severe allergy, please contact our team before ordering so we can advise on the safest options.
We carry a selected range of sugar-free and eggless cakes that are freshly prepared to order. Look for the dietary labels on each product page, or reach out to us for a tailored recommendation for your celebration.
Cream and cheesecake products should be kept refrigerated and enjoyed within 2–3 days. Dry oriental sweets such as baklava and maamoul keep well at room temperature for up to a week in a sealed container, away from direct sunlight.
We accept Visa, Mastercard and Meeza cards, mobile wallets, and cash on delivery. All online payments are processed through a secure, encrypted gateway, and your card details are never stored on our servers.
