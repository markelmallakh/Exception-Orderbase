The Story Behind Our Signature Baklava

Blog
03 June 2025 6 mins reading By Chef Hana Adel

Few sweets carry as much history as baklava. At Exception, our recipe has been passed down and perfected for generations — a delicate balance of paper-thin filo, roasted nuts and fragrant sugar syrup that has become one of the most requested trays in every branch we open.

A Recipe Rooted in Tradition

It begins with the dough. Each sheet of filo is stretched until it is almost translucent, then brushed with clarified butter and layered — sometimes more than thirty sheets deep. Between the layers we scatter generous handfuls of pistachios and walnuts, toasted that morning so their aroma is at its peak.

The Syrup Makes the Difference

Once baked to a deep golden brown, the trays are finished with a warm syrup infused with orange blossom and a whisper of lemon. Poured at exactly the right moment, the syrup soaks through every layer without ever making the pastry heavy — the mark of a baklava done properly.

Crafted Fresh, Every Single Day

We never let baklava sit. Every tray is cut, baked and finished in-house so that when it reaches your table it still carries the crackle of fresh filo. It is a small promise, but it is the reason our customers keep coming back — and the reason a box of Exception baklava still feels like a celebration.

More Than a Sweet

For us, baklava is a way of sharing a piece of Egyptian hospitality. Whether it marks a wedding, an Eid gathering or an ordinary afternoon with tea, we are proud that our family recipe continues to bring people together — one golden, syrup-soaked bite at a time.
