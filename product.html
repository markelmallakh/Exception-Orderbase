The Exception Signature

Our Chocolate Fudge Cake is built on three layers of deeply moist Belgian chocolate sponge, brushed with sugar syrup and generously filled with a velvety chocolate fudge. Every cake is finished by hand with a mirror-glaze ganache and delicate chocolate shavings.

Made fresh to order in our Cairo kitchens using the same family recipe we have perfected over the decades.

  • Three layers of Belgian chocolate sponge

Delivery & Storage

Same-day delivery is available across Greater Cairo for orders placed before 4:00 PM. Nationwide delivery arrives within 1–2 business days in insulated packaging.

  • Keep refrigerated at 2–5°C
  • Best enjoyed within 3 days
  • Bring to room temperature 20 minutes before serving
