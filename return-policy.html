Return Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

This Return Policy is a placeholder template describing how we handle refunds and replacements for our freshly prepared products. Because our cakes and sweets are perishable food items, our approach focuses on quality and freshness rather than traditional returns. Please replace this text with your finalised, legally reviewed policy before going live.

1. Our Freshness Guarantee

Every order is baked and packed with care so that it reaches you in perfect condition. If your product arrives damaged, spoiled or not as described, we will make it right with a replacement or refund.

2. Eligibility for Refunds & Replacements

You may be eligible for a refund or replacement in the following cases:

  • The product arrived damaged or in unsatisfactory condition.
  • You received the wrong item or an incomplete order.
  • The product quality did not meet our published standards.

3. Non-Returnable Items

As our products are freshly made and perishable, we are unable to accept returns simply due to a change of mind. Custom and personalised cakes cannot be refunded once production has begun, except in the cases listed above.

4. How to Report an Issue

Please contact our customer care team within 24 hours of receiving your order. To help us resolve your request quickly, kindly provide:

  • Your order number and the date of delivery.
  • A short description of the issue.
  • Clear photos of the product and its packaging, where possible.

5. Refund Method & Timelines

Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method or, where you prefer, as store credit to your wallet. Card refunds are typically processed within 7 to 14 business days, depending on your bank.

6. Cancelled or Missed Deliveries

If a delivery cannot be completed because no one was available at the address, we will attempt to contact you to reschedule. Additional delivery charges may apply for repeat attempts.

7. Contact Us

For any questions about refunds, replacements or the status of a request, please reach out to our team through the contact page and we will be glad to assist.
