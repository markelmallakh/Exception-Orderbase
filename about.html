Our Story

A Legacy of Taste

What began as a single, modest bakery in the heart of Cairo has grown into one of Egypt's most beloved names in oriental sweets and celebration cakes. For more than six decades, Exception has stayed true to the family recipes that started it all — pairing the finest local ingredients with the patience and craft that only time can teach. From glistening trays of baklava and kunafa to towering gateaux for life's happiest moments, every bite carries the same promise we have always kept: nothing but the best for our guests.
